 AuburnHacks Prompt

In 2025, a medical crisis broke out at the annual Innovation City Tech Fair. Hundreds of civilians were injured in a massive earthquake, and many lost their lives due to traffic overload and a lack in medical resources.
To be more readily prepared for future incidents, the city council is looking for solutions that will improve or re-imagine city infrastructure through either transportation or healthcare. In your team, design and implement a new concept that will help Innovation City live up to its name. Any solution from software to hardware is welcome, as long as it is creative and original work!
